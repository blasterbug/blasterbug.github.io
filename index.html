Greetings!

I am a freelance developer living at Umeå, north Sweden. Back to September 2015, I came as an exchange student from France to study Computer Science at Umeå University. I picked subjects such as Artificial Intelligence, Distributed Systems and Parallel Computing. Then I worked for five month with Thingmap.

I like to code with C and Python but my skills are larger. During my spare time, I enjoy cooking vegetarian dishes and build funny stuff with my Arduino Uno. I also used to work as a volunteer. I would like to join a Sea Shepherd compaign because I really feel concerned about environmental issues.
